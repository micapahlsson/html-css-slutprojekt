Our packages

Kiruna – The Northern Lights Package

Price: from 4,990 SEK per person

Experience magical nights under the Northern Lights!

Stay at the famous Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, ride a husky sled across the snowy plains, and end the evening with a three-course dinner in the Ice Bar. An unforgettable nature experience in Sweden's northernmost town!

Includes: 2 nights at a hotel with breakfast, husky sled tour, and Northern Lights chase.

Stockholm – Archipelago Adventure

Price: from 3,490 SEK per person

A summer weekend in the heart of the archipelago

Cruise between the islands, paddle a kayak, and enjoy freshly caught fish by the sea. Relax at a cozy guesthouse in Sandhamn and experience the most beautiful sides of Stockholm through boating, swimming, and nature.

Includes: 2 nights at a hotel with breakfast, boat excursion, kayaking tour, and dinner at a seaside restaurant.

Åre – The Winter Package

Price: from 5,290 SEK per person

Skiing, spa, and après-ski in a mountain setting

Three days filled with skiing, spa relaxation, and delicious food. Stay at Åre Mountain Lodge with full board, unwind in the sauna, and enjoy the best après-ski in the Jämtland mountains.

Includes: 3 nights at a hotel with breakfast, ski pass, breakfast & dinner, and spa entry.

Gothenburg - The Family Package

Price: from 3,190 SEK per person

Fun-filled days for the whole family!

Visit Liseberg amusement park, Universeum science center, and the city aquarium. End the day with shopping in Nordstan and dinner along Avenyn. The perfect package for families looking to combine fun and relaxation.

Includes: 2 nights at a hotel, entrance to Liseberg & Universeum, and breakfast buffet.
