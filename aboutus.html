❀ About Triply Sweden ❀

“Travel with ease, leave the planning to us !”

Tina Jones founded Triply in 2017 after visiting Sweden and
not finding enough trip advisors to help her fulfill her desire.
She wanted to explore Sweden in the way she knew she could. This
inspired her to come together with her 5 employees, and
now dear friends, to create a space where travelers, as well as
locals, could find fun ideas and pre planned out trips to
make the most of their time in Sweden!

Tina Jones -
"Sweden might be a small country, but it's filled with
beauty and so much to offer. I want tourists, and even locals,
to see more of what the country has to offer. This is why I
started Triply Sweden!"
